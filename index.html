After a period of more than 10 successful years in exhibition management with a strong focus on the fashion and lifestyle industry, One Management International proudly brings to you yet another feather in its cap, ‘The London Market App’.

The app is based on the simple concept of ‘Affordable Luxury’, where we strive to present creative and beautiful products from sellers across the world, through an intelligent and user-friendly app. We believe in the power of expressing oneself, as we aspire to showcase products and brands that stand apart. About

After a period of more than 10 successful years in exhibition management with a strong focus on the fashion and lifestyle industry, One Management International proudly brings to you yet another feather in its cap, ‘The London Market App’.

The app is based on the simple concept of ‘Affordable Luxury’, where we strive to present creative and beautiful products from sellers across the world, through an intelligent and user-friendly app. We believe in the power of expressing oneself, as we aspire to showcase products and brands that stand apart. With our wide array of products ranging from clothing and accessories to home décor and furniture, we have something unique for everyone.

OUR APP

We invite you to join our new shopping experience "London Market App". This new concept is based on the mantra- 'Affordable Luxury' which is time- saving, unique, extremely productive as well as very very very user-friendly. London Market would present the most creative brands & beautiful products from all over the world in your phone through an intelligent & smart user- friendly app showcasing;

Women clothing | Children clothing | Menswear | Accessories | Jewellery | Footwear | Home Decor & Linen | Handicrafts | Furniture | Beauty | Flowers & Gifting | F&B | Customised Tailoring | Books & Stationary | Health & Safety

Who are we?

We are in the exhibition management profession for more than 10 years in India, and have successful exhibition brands called 'London Market', 'CandyPOPS', 'Princess Day Out', 'Magique for Arte`’. We organize 11 Fashion exhibitions each year having a client base of more than 5000 Fashion Designers with categories of pret, couture, footwear, jewellery, stoles, scarves, bags, home linen & decor. Our exhibitions are well known among the fashion community.
